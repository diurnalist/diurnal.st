Summary

After four years working up and down the stack at SoundCloud, I'd like to contribute my expertise to a company or team solving problems on a larger scale. My current (admittedly varied) interest areas are high-level and enterprise architecture, dev-ops, and infrastructure and network security.

Work for this team introduced me to Scala, and I soon moved in to a backend role and helped both with building microservices from the ground-up and maintaining legacy code. I decided to eventually transition to the security team to follow my passion for the challenges in that field.
